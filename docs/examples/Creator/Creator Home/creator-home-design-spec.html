Layout

Screen structure

The creator home is a single vertical scroll. Six ordered sections appear in this sequence:

  1. Greeting — always present
  2. Blocker — 0 or 1 card (conditional)
  3. Your Collabs — always present
  4. Earnings — always present
  5. Community Love — conditional (shown once love is received)
  6. Footer Links — always present

Outside the scroll area: iOS status bar (top), home-indicator tab bar (bottom). Those are already in the DS — don't redesign them.

Section 1

Greeting

What it is: The header text at the top of every home screen. Creator's name + a short contextual status line.

What to design: One component with adaptive copy. Structure stays identical; only the two text strings change.

Structure

  • Greeting line (Title/X-Large, serif): large name + punctuation
  • Status sub-line (Body/Large, sans): one sentence describing the moment
  • Notifications bell icon button on the right (reuse DS button-icon)

Section 2

Blocker

What it is: A single prominent card that surfaces the most important action the creator needs to take. Replaces the old "next step" concept — the blocker IS the next step.

What to design: A reusable card with: icon (colored 40×40 tile) + title + subtitle + full-width pill CTA button.

Structure

  • Card: white bg, 1px DS border, 16px radius, 18px padding
  • Top row: icon tile + title/subtitle stack
  • Bottom: orange pill CTA button spanning full width

Section 3

Your Collabs

What it is: Shows the state of the creator's collabs. Title is always "Your collabs"; the subtitle communicates count/state. Tapping any card goes to the collab details screen.

What to design: Multiple card layouts, used individually or in combination depending on how many collabs exist and what state they're in.

3a. Collab card — themed

Structure: A rich card with a photo hero, gradient overlay, italic collab name, date range, and a stats block below on a colored accent band. Four "themes" (purple, orange, red, teal) — these are creator-customized colors, not DS tokens. Treat them as runtime-configurable.

3b. Draft carousel

Structure: Horizontal scroll of 3 themed collab cards (cards from 3a). A "Your pick" star badge appears on the first/recommended card. Carousel dots below.

What to design: Just the carousel container + dot indicators. The cards themselves are 3a.

3c. Active collab carousel (full-width snap)

Structure: Full-width horizontal scroll for when 2+ collabs are active. Each slide shows the full live card (3a "Live" sub-state) at screen width. Dots below.

3d. Past collab row

Structure: Compact single-row list item for completed collabs. White card, 1px border, 16px radius.

  • Left: 48×48 square photo, 12px radius
  • Middle: collab name (Playfair italic, 16px) + meta line ("Date range · X participants")
  • Right: earned amount (orange) + "earned" label + chevron

Section 4

Earnings

What it is: Cross-collab money summary. Always present. White card with a large orange hero total. Tappable when the creator has earnings (accruing or earned modes) — taps go to earnings history in the profile.

What to design: One card component with three content variations.

Structure

  • White card, 1px DS border, 12px radius, 20px padding
  • Header row: icon + label (small uppercase caption) on the left, chevron on the right
  • Total amount: Headline Large (46px serif), orange/100 — the largest size in the DS, treated as a hero number
  • Below the total: either a subtitle line (projected mode) or a 2-column breakdown row (accruing/earned)

4a. Projected mode

  • Label: "Earning potential" with trending_up icon
  • Chevron in header (visual consistency with other modes — but tap behavior is up to dev; no earnings history exists yet)
  • Total: estimated amount (e.g. $729)
  • Below total: "estimated per collab with 1% of your followers" (body/small secondary)

4b. Accruing mode (tappable)

  • Label: "Estimated earnings" with trending_up icon
  • Chevron in header — entire card is tappable, goes to earnings history in profile
  • Total: sum of past + current (e.g. $1,343)
  • Below total: 2-column breakdown with thin divider between
    • Column 1: value (14px bold) + "Past" label
    • Column 2: value (14px bold) + "Est. current" label

4c. Earned mode (tappable)

  • Label: "Your earnings" with trending_up icon
  • Chevron in header — entire card is tappable, goes to earnings history in profile
  • Total: past earnings total
  • Below total: same 2-column breakdown
    • Past: earned amount
    • Est. current: $0
Section 5

Community Love

What it is: Aggregated appreciation from participants across all collabs. Motivation signal.

What to design: One card with a hidden state (no design needed) and a visible state.

Visible state structure

  • White card, 1px DS border, 16px radius, 18px padding
  • Header row: title "Your community loves you" (title/small serif) + heart icon + total count
  • Emoji breakdown row: 4 emoji categories with count + label, thin dividers between
    • 🔥 Fired up
    • 💪 So worth it
    • 💜 All the feels
    • 🌱 Growing
  • Featured quote block: orange/0 bg, 8px radius, italic quote + attribution (— Name · @handle)
  • Bottom link: "Read all messages →"

Hidden state: Section simply doesn't render. No design needed.

Section 6

Footer Links

What it is: Two low-priority persistent links at the bottom of every home screen. No variations — always identical.

What to design: Two card components, stacked.

6a. Invite nudge

  • Subtle orange gradient bg (orange/30 to warm cream), 1px orange/60 border, 16px radius
  • 36×36 icon tile (orange tinted bg) with person_add icon
  • Copy: "Know a creator who'd be great on Motiv?" (body/medium)
  • Text link CTA: "Send an invite" (orange/100)

6b. Creator community link

  • White card, 1px DS border, 16px radius
  • 36×36 purple-tinted icon tile with forum icon
  • Two-line text: "Creator Community" + "Tips, support, and creator connections"
  • Chevron on right
Guardrails

DS compliance notes

  • Colors: Use only DS tokens. The four collab card theme colors (purple/orange/red/teal overlays) are creator-specific runtime colors, not DS tokens — keep them as variables.
  • Typography: Use only the DS type scale. Sizes: 12px (Body/Small, Caption), 14px (Body/Medium), 16px (Body/Large, Title/Small), 20px (Title/Medium), 24px (Title/Large), 32px (Title/X-Large), 46px (Headline Large).
  • Spacing: Use only DS spacing tokens: 0, 2, 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 36, 56.
  • Border radius: Use only DS tokens: 2, 4, 8, 12, 16, 999 (pill).
  • Icons: Material Icons throughout.
