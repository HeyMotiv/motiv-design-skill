About this Collab

This challenge is designed to push you beyond your comfort zone while building sustainable fitness habits. You'll work with personalized goals based on your current performance level.

Game Rules & FAQs
1
Hit your weekly goal
Complete the required output and ride frequency each week to stay on track.
2
Log rides via Peloton sync
Your rides are automatically tracked through your connected Peloton account.
3
Miss a week, you're out
If you don't meet the minimum goal in any week, you're eliminated from the pool.
4
Finishers split the pool
Everyone who completes all 5 weeks splits the collab pool equally.
What happens to my $40 entry hold?
Your entry hold is returned in full when you meet your collab goals. If you don't, it goes into the reward pool for those who do.
Can I pause for a week?
No — consistency is the point. Every participant must meet the weekly goal each week to remain eligible.
How is the pool divided?
The total pool is split equally among all participants who complete every week of the collab.
What if I get injured mid-collab?
We understand things happen. Reach out to support and we'll review your case on an individual basis.
