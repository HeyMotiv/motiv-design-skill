What happens to my $40 Motiv?expand_more
Your Motiv is returned in full when you hit your goals. If you miss, you forfeit it — but you keep participating.
Can I pause for a week?expand_more
No — consistency is the point. Every participant must meet the weekly goal each week to remain eligible.
How is the pool divided?expand_more
The rewards pool is split equally among all participants who hit their weekly goals.
What if I get injured?expand_more
We understand things happen. Reach out to support and we'll review your case on an individual basis.
