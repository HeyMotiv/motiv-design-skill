FAQ & Game rules expand_more
How does the entry hold work?

When you join, your entry amount is held securely. Complete the challenge to get it back plus bonus rewards from the prize pool.

What happens if I miss a day?

Missing a day forfeits a portion of your entry hold. Stay consistent to maximize your rewards.

Can I invite friends?

Yes! Share your unique invite link to bring friends into the challenge. Group challenges are more fun together.

Game Rules
  • Complete the required activity each day during the challenge window
  • Log your activity through the app before midnight
  • Rest days, if any, will be noted in the schedule
  • All participants must follow community guidelines
