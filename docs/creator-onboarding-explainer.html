How It Works

The 5-Step Onboarding Flow

From Instagram handle to guaranteed earnings — each step personalizes the experience based on how the creator connects with their audience.

1

Instagram Handle

Creator enters their public Instagram URL. System pulls lightweight public metadata: handle, display name, profile photo, follower tier.

2

Archetype Classification

Based on follower tier, engagement band, and content patterns, the system classifies the creator into one of six archetypes. The archetype is never shown explicitly — it only informs what the creator sees.

3

Narrative Reveal

Two personalized screens show the creator why their community connects with them. Copy feels personal because it reflects how creators at their scale typically resonate.

4

Engagement Diagnosis

2-3 self-reported questions about where audience engagement stalls. Based on answers, creators are placed into State 1, 2, or 3.

5

Collab Solution + Earnings

The system shows how running a collab directly addresses their specific friction point, with guaranteed earnings of $350 when their collab goes live.

Revenue

Updated Earnings Model

Updated Earnings Model

Previous Creators could earn ~$10-15K per quarter from running 5-week collabs.

Proposed

  • $350 guaranteed when a collab goes live (activated = minimum participants joined)
  • Referral bonuses for driving sign-ups (e.g., milestone at 1,000 followers)
  • Revenue share from participant activity

Total potential earnings are roughly the same range, but the guaranteed floor puts creators at ease.

Key change in onboarding copy: We now lead with "you will absolutely get paid for getting a collab live" rather than a quarterly projection.
